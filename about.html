Origin Story

Flagship store: New Jersey

The idea of this restaurant came to me when I really wanted Basil fried rice and had nowhere to get it. I found a recipe online and it turned out amazing. I started bringing the dish to house gatherings and it was a hit. After that, a friend told me I should consider opening up a restaurant. And Now I have created one of the top rated thai restaurants in the US and Europe.
